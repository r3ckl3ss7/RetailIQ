### Problem Statement
Many local shop owners and small vendors still rely on handwritten diaries or unorganized billing methods to track sales. Because of this, they struggle to monitor revenue, identify top-selling products, analyze seasonal trends, and make informed business decisions. Existing software solutions are often too complex, expensive, or not designed for small-scale businesses.

This project builds an AI-powered sales and analytics platform that simplifies billing, digitizes records, and enables conversational business insights for small retailers.
